Process Overview

End-to-end visibility across every collection cycle

01
Farmer
Morning Dispatch Signal
Farmers log milk volume via SMS or the MilkTracker mobile app. The system pre-assigns the nearest available collection vehicle and optimises its route.
02
In Transit
Live Route Optimisation
GPS telemetry streams to the control centre every 30 seconds. The AI reroutes vehicles around delays and balances collection sequences for maximum cold-chain integrity.
03
Factory Gate
Automated Intake & Logging
On arrival, RFID tags trigger automatic quality sampling records and volume verification. Farmer payouts are calculated and queued within minutes of each delivery.
